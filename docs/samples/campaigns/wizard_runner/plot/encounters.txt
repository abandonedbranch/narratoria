# Challenge Archetypes\n\nEach room presents a challenge from one of these core archetypes. Phi-4-mini will generate unique variations while preserving the archetype's core tension.\n\n## Puzzle Challenges\n\n**Core Tension**: Logic vs. Intuition  \n**Victory Condition**: Solve the pattern; unlock the door\n\n*Examples*: Symbol arrangement, logic gates, spatial reasoning, lateral thinking\n\n---\n\n## Courage Challenges\n\n**Core Tension**: Fear vs. Progress  \n**Victory Condition**: Face the threat; claim the passage\n\n*Examples*: Combat encounter, physical gauntlet, psychological trial, moral choice\n\n---\n\n## Agility Challenges\n\n**Core Tension**: Speed vs. Precision  \n**Victory Condition**: Navigate the obstacle while maintaining integrity\n\n*Examples*: Timed sequence, platforming, environmental hazard, dexterity test\n\n---\n\n## Integrity Challenges\n\n**Core Tension**: Temptation vs. Purpose  \n**Victory Condition**: Remain true to your values despite the cost\n\n*Examples*: Moral dilemma, sacrifice choice, deception offered, truth vs. comfort\n\n---\n\n## Synthesis Challenge (Final Room)\n\n**Core Tension**: Everything you've learned vs. your deepest limitation  \n**Victory Condition**: Integrate all lessons and choose your own path\n\nThe final challenge does not have a single correct solution. It rewards creativity, growth, and the player's unique choices throughout the campaign.\n"
