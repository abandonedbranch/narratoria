# Wizard Runner

The player is tasked by a wizard to run through a series of rooms, each containing a different challenge. The player must complete each challenge to progress to the next room and ultimately reach the end of the floor, where they will find a treasure chest. The player must use their skills and wits to overcome the obstacles in each room and claim the treasure at the end. Once treasure is claimed the player returns to the wizard to report their success and receive a reward for their efforts. The campaign is designed to test the player's problem-solving abilities, agility, and combat skills as they navigate through the various challenges presented by the wizard. Each room will have a unique theme and set of obstacles, ranging from puzzles and traps to combat encounters with monsters. The player must use their resources wisely and think strategically to overcome each challenge and reach the end of the floor. The campaign is suitable for players of all levels and can be customized to fit the preferences of the group.
